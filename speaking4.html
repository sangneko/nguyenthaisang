Part 2: Topic – Smartphone

I want to talk about the smartphone. It’s a useful invention that many people use today.

A smartphone is small, easy to carry, and has many functions. We can call, text, take photos, listen to music, and watch videos.

It helps students learn better and workers to do their jobs.

During COVID-19, smartphones supported online learning and working.

Apps like Google Maps and YouTube save time and connect people.

However, overusing phones can harm our health.

In short, smartphones make life easier and more convenient.

Part 3: Further Questions

  • 1. How has it changed people’s lives?
    It helps people work, study, and connect.
  • 2. Any bad effects?
    Yes. It can hurt our eyes and reduce face-to-face communication.
  • 3. Who benefits the most?
    Students and workers benefit most from smartphones.
